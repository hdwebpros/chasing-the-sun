# Triad Review — *Chasing the Sun*

Every triad in the current Drive manuscript, enumerated and ranked worst-first.
A **triad** is acoustic: a reader *hears* three matched beats between a clause and
a stop (variety `SKILL.md`, items 9–14). Three shapes are detected:

- **COMMA** — `A, B, and C` / `A, B, or C` (syndetic) or `A, B, C.` (asyndetic)
- **FRAGSTK** — three consecutive short fragments (`Dry. Clean. No mold.`)
- **OPENER** — three consecutive sentences opening with the same word (`He… He… He…`)

## Two caveats before you fix anything

1. **The scanner finds triads; it can't tell a grief ritual from a market trip.**
   Per `SKILL.md`'s keep test, emotional-peak SVO runs (death scenes), anaphora that
   lands on a *withheld turn* or a *concrete reversal*, and signature motifs are
   **keeps, not tics**. Several Tier-S hits are exactly those. Judge each against
   `VOICE.md` before touching Drive.
2. **A run of four is sanctioned, three is the trap** ("two blunt beats, or four —
   never the smooth matched three"). The scanner already excludes 4+ builds
   (`A man. A bench. A valley. An empty seat.` is *not* flagged) and 4-item comma
   lists (`No mother, no father, no uncle, no cousin.` is *not* flagged).
